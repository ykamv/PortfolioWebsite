Ikigai Studios has been at the forefront of the motion picture and animation industry since its inception and continues to be a leading creative force in the industry.

Ikigai Studios produces and distributes a wide-ranging slate of TV Shows and Anime each year. Ikigai Studio either fully finances or co-finances the films it produces and maintains worldwide distribution rights.

Among Ikigai Studio's vast Portfolio are major names such as Attack on Titan, Jujutsu Kaisen, Game of Thrones, Peaky Blinders.
